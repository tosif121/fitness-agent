Working out at home sounds great. Until you realize... nobody is watching your form.
You could be destroying your knees with bad squats, and you'd never know.
What if your webcam could actually coach you? In real time?

Meet FitAgent. A real-time AI personal trainer that lives entirely in your browser.
No app downloads. No wearable sensors. No expensive gym memberships.
Just open your webcam, and your AI coach is ready.

Under the hood, FitAgent uses three powerful technologies working together.
First, YOLO eleven pose detection tracks seventeen key points on your body at thirty frames per second.
Second, a deterministic physics engine analyzes your joint angles to count every single rep, instantly.
And third, Google Gemini Realtime acts as your voice coach. It sees your movement, hears your voice, and talks back to you. Live.

FitAgent can auto-detect any exercise you start performing. Whether it's push-ups, jumping jacks, or lunges, it adapts instantly. 
Let me show you how it works with a simple example: Squats.
Watch what happens when I start my first set.

The AI instantly recognizes the movement, creates a tracker, and starts counting my reps. No buttons pressed. No manual data entry.
It's analyzing the angle of my hips relative to my knees in real time to ensure I hit proper depth.

And here's where it gets really cool.
The AI coach isn't just counting. It's watching my form and speaking to me in real time.
It corrects my posture, motivates me, and assigns a live form score. All through natural voice conversation. It's like having a trainer right in the room.
